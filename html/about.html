ABOUT US

PBB: Pinoy Best Bites, established in 1999, brings the warmth and fun of the "Bahay ni Kuya" to every Filipino table. Inspired by Pinoy
Big Brother, our restaurant celebrates food, stories, and shared moments.

From adobo and sinigang to lechon kawali and halo-halo, each dish delivers a taste of home — comforting, flavorful, and
unforgettable. At PBB, the kitchen is the confession room, the table is where stories unfold, and every meal is a winning bite —
panalong sarap, sulit sa bawat kain.

More than food, we're about joy, camaraderie, and pride in serving the best of Filipino cuisine. Every plate feels like a big family
reunion, full of flavor, laughter, and memories.

VISION

To be the most-loved Filipino restaurant that brings people together through authentic
flavors, heartfelt moments, and the true spirit of "home."

MISSION

At PBB: Pinoy Best Bites, we serve more than just food — we serve stories, memories, and
the warmth of Filipino hospitality. We are committed to celebrating our culture through
every bite, creating a space where families, friends, and food lovers can connect, share,
and feel at home.

PBB SERVICES

DINE-IN

Enjoy the comfort of home-style dining in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. Perfect for families, barkadas, and everyone who loves authentic Filipino food.

TAKE-OUT & DELIVERY

Craving PBB flavors on the go? Order your favorites for take-out or have them delivered right to your doorstep — same panalong sarap, anytime, anywhere!

WEDDINGS & SPECIAL EVENTS

Make your celebrations extra special with our catering and event packages. From weddings and birthdays to reunions and corporate gatherings — we serve happiness on every plate.

PRIVATE FUNCTIONS

Reserve our space for your exclusive gatherings! Ideal for small parties, team meetings, or intimate celebrations with your closest circle.

CATERING SERVICE

Let us bring the PBB experience to you! Our full-service catering delivers the same authentic taste and warmth of Filipino hospitality wherever you are.
