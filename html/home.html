INDULGING WITH HISTORY

PBB: Pinoy Best Bites was founded with the passion to bring the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine closer to every table. What started as a simple idea of celebrating local dishes has now grown into one of the most authentic restaurants in town, known for serving comfort food that feels like home. Rooted in tradition yet open to innovation, PBB continues to honor Filipino culinary heritage while creating new food memories for every guest.

AWARDS

Through the years, PBB has been recognized for its excellence, earning accolades such as the “Best Local Dining Experience” (2021) and “Top Filipino Restaurant in Central Luzon” (2023). It was also featured in the Foodie Choice Awards for its signature kare-kare and innovative takes on classic Pinoy street food. Today, PBB continues to serve not just meals, but stories—each dish carrying the essence of tradition, family, 'and the unmistakable taste of home.

FEATURED ON

The restaurant has been proudly featured on national food and lifestyle magazines, highlighted in “Taste of the North” travel guides, and showcased in popular TV segments such as “Kusina Pilipinas” and “Food Finds PH”. These features celebrate not only its authentic flavors but also its commitment to promoting Filipino food culture.
